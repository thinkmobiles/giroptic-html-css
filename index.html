The most versatile camera ever made

From outdoor activities to personal and professional live-steaming sessions, the unique GIROPTIC 360cam modular base system will allow you to use your camera just the way you want.

  • Battery
  • Lightbud
  • Ethernet Streaming

Made for adventures on the go, the battery base holds a rechargeable and removable Li-ion battery providing up to an hour of video recording. The regular mount thread allow the 360cam to be used with any regular camera accessories and mounts (tripods, selfie sticks, suction cups…) while the Micro USB port allow file transfers and battery recharges.

Set up a home surveillance system to keep an eye on the kids or the pets in the next room without the messy cables. The 360cam WiFi capability allows real-time streaming to your mobile device or cloud video surveillance service, while the lightbulb base will provide constant and direct power to your camera.

For professional broadcasterswho want to stream unique live events, or professional video surveillance for24/7 360° view. In environment where wireless can be unreliable, the POEcompatible Ethernet streaming base will allow steady and high-speed connection.Professional video streaming at full throttle!
